Welcome to Potions!

The meanest professor, Head Of Slytherin House Severus Snape teaches potions. Severus Snape was the Half Blood Prince who made the famous annotations in the potions textbook.
Horace Slughorn replaced Snape as the potions master when Snape started teaching DADA.
Some famous potions are:

  • Draught of Living Death
  • Felix Felicis(Liquid Luck)
  • Draught of Peace
  • Polyjuice Potion
  • Amortentia(Love Potion)
